About us

Hilla Inc. Trucking Company has the most skilled and dedicated team of professional drivers on the road. With the experience of many years in the business, our drivers are our most valued assets and the reason for our growth and success.

Hilla Inc. Trucking Company is committed to improving the quality of life for our drivers and their families by providing the best equipment, superior pay, high-quality benefits, and ensuring efficient operations and load planning that allow them to maximize their home life without sacrificing safety or income.

We operate routes throughout the contiguous 48 states, so home is always just a day or two away. At Hilla Inc. Trucking Company, drivers have the freedom to choose. We provide the job that best fits our drivers lifestyle.

Behind the scenes, the finest staff of customer service, logistics, driver managers, maintenance, and human resource professionals in the industry supports our drivers. We never forget that our success depends on our drivers success.
